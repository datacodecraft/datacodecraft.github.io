Local School Hosts Green Week Festival

This week, a local school in Izmir launched its annual Green Week Festival, focusing on environmental awareness and sustainability. Students, teachers, and families came together to participate in recycling workshops, tree planting activities, and educational exhibits.

The event has attracted attention from nearby communities, with many visitors praising the effort. One student said, We are learning how small actions can make a big difference for our planet. The festival also included handmade crafts and eco-friendly product displays created by students.

Community Response

Organizers reported a strong turnout and positive feedback from attendees. Many parents expressed excitement about the program and hope it will continue to grow each year. Environmental education was highlighted as the main goal of the event.

“If we take care of nature today, it will take care of us tomorrow.”

The school principal added, Izmir Education Bulletin, that the festival helps students connect classroom learning with real-world responsibility. Plans are already being discussed for next year’s event.
